{{ page.desc }}

This faux online merch store is loosely based around the anime series Attack on Titan, featuring details related to its world and characters. I found the idea of designing a product landing page for various fictional gadgets to be an intriguing one. I also thought using the world of Attack on Titan in particular would create somewhat of an absurd juxtaposition, if only because of how far removed the internet (let alone eCommerce) is from that story.
