For my second-ever web development project, I again wanted to incorporate one of my personal interests. Given the task of designing a basic tribute webpage that pays homage to some famous figure or group, I ultimately landed on Radiohead, one of my favorite bands. This decision also helped inform some of the page's content, particularly the discography listing.
